# 📊 Online Retail Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Online Retail Data Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive **Tableau** dashboard developed to analyze global sales performance, revenue distribution, customer purchasing behavior, and time-based trends.
This dashboard provides actionable insights into business performance across countries and time periods, enabling data-driven decision-making through dynamic and intuitive visualizations.

## 🎯 Business Objective
The objective of this dashboard is to:
- Monitor overall revenue, quantity, and pricing metrics.
- Analyze monthly order and quantity trends.
- Evaluate global sales distribution across countries.
- Identify top-performing markets.
- Understand weekly sales performance patterns.
- Support strategic business and marketing decisions.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Tableau

- Data Cleaning
- Data Analysis

- Dashboard Design

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Interactive filter for:
  - Country

- KPI Cards displaying:
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Quantity
  - Total Unit Price Value

- Monthly Order & Quantity Trend Analysis
- Global Sales & Revenue Map Visualization
- Weekly Sales Performance Breakdown
- Revenue by Country (Bubble Chart)
- Revenue Share of Top Countries (Pie Chart)
- Dynamic cross-filtering across all visualizations

## 📈 Key Insights
### Overall Performance
- **Total Revenue:** ₹5.17M+
- **Total Quantity:** 722K+
- **Total Unit Price Value:** ₹2.49M+
- Strong sales volume with consistent order activity.
- Revenue and quantity show a positive correlation.

### Monthly Trends
- Sales gradually increased throughout the year.
- Peak performance was observed around **November 2011**.
- A noticeable decline occurred in **December**, which may indicate seasonal effects or incomplete data.

### Weekly Performance
- **Thursday** and **Tuesday** generated the highest revenue (approximately ₹2M).
- **Sunday** recorded the lowest sales (approximately ₹1M).
- Weekday sales consistently outperformed weekend sales.

### Geographic Performance
- The **United Kingdom** contributed **93.73%** of total revenue.
- Other notable contributors include:
  - Netherlands: **3.26%**
  - EIRE: **3.01%**
- Revenue is heavily concentrated in a single market.

### Global Distribution
- Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of countries.
- Countries such as **Australia** and **Singapore** contribute moderate revenue.
- Significant opportunities exist for international market expansion.

### Country-Level Insights
- The bubble chart highlights the **United Kingdom** as the dominant revenue-generating market.
- Other countries contribute considerably smaller shares.
- Revenue distribution is highly imbalanced across regions.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Reduce dependency on the UK market by expanding into new international markets.
- Increase marketing efforts in underperforming countries.
- Schedule promotional campaigns during high-performing weekdays.
- Improve weekend sales through targeted offers and discounts.
- Investigate the December sales decline to identify seasonal or operational factors.
- Strengthen business presence in emerging markets to diversify revenue sources.
